These are some of the folks we've had the pleasure to work with over the years. They have graciously given us permission to put their stuff up on this site as examples of our work in a variety of styles.

All songs recorded, mixed, and mastered by Dave Whitcomb at SDRC unless otherwise noted. All files are in ALAC (Lossless) format. Click on a title to play. Songs will open in a new window.

-- If you are having trouble playing the tracks on your device, we've also uploaded all of them to SoundCloud. Click on the link to go to the page: https://soundcloud.com/straydogrecordingco/tracks

HOWLING POPPIES, New Mexico. Indie art pop.
